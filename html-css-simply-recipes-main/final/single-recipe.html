Banana Pancakes

Fluffy, golden pancakes infused with the natural sweetness of ripe bananas. Each bite is soft and moist, with a delicate balance of banana flavor and a light, airy texture. Topped with extra slices of fresh banana, a drizzle of maple syrup, and a dusting of powdered sugar, these pancakes are the perfect way to start your morning. The combination of warm pancakes and sweet banana makes for a comforting and indulgent breakfast treat. Serve with a dollop of whipped cream or a sprinkle of cinnamon for an extra touch of decadence. instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Bananas

Mash the ripe bananas in a bowl until smooth. Set aside. This will provide the natural sweetness and moisture to the pancakes.

Step 2: Mix the Wet Ingredients

In a separate bowl, whisk together eggs, milk, vanilla extract, and the mashed bananas until well combined.

Step 3: Prepare the Dry Ingredients and Combine

In another bowl, mix flour, baking powder, and a pinch of salt. Slowly add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture and stir until just combined. Be careful not to overmix.

Step 4: Cook the Pancakes

Heat a non-stick pan or griddle over medium heat. Pour a scoop of batter onto the pan for each pancake. Cook for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until golden brown.

Step 5: Serve and Enjoy!

Serve the pancakes warm with sliced bananas, maple syrup, and a dusting of powdered sugar for a perfect breakfast treat.
